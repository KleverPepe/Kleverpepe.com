Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about KleverPEPE

What is KleverPEPE?

+
KleverPEPE is a community-driven meme token built on the Klever blockchain. It combines DeFi staking rewards with play-to-earn gaming partnerships, offering real utility beyond the typical meme coin experience.

What is the token contract address?

+
The KPEPE token contract address is KPEPE-1EOD on KleverChain. You can view it on Kleverscan.

How does staking work?

+
Stake your KPEPE tokens in the Klever Wallet to earn 50% APR. Minimum stake is 0, with a 24-epoch lock period before you can unstake. After unstaking, there's a 4-epoch withdrawal delay.

Where can I buy KPEPE?

+
You can buy KPEPE on these decentralized exchanges: Digiko, Same DEX, Vox Swap, and Swopus.

Is KleverPEPE audited?

+
Yes, the KPEPE smart contract is deployed on KleverChain, which has been audited and operates with transparent governance. The staking mechanism is built on Klever's native staking protocol.

What makes KleverPEPE different?

+
Unlike most meme coins, KleverPEPE offers real utility through: 1) High staking rewards (50% APR), 2) Play-to-earn gaming partnerships, 3) Strong community governance, 4) Built on the fast and cheap Klever blockchain.
