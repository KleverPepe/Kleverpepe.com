‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't this be automated?
Klever's ecosystem currently doesn't provide stable APIs for automated contract interaction from Node.js. The most reliable method is using KleverScan's web interface with your wallet.
Is it safe to use KleverScan?
Yes, KleverScan is the official block explorer for KleverChain. It's safe and widely used. Your wallet connection is handled securely by the Klever Wallet app on your device.
How much does it cost?
Transaction fee: ~3 KLV. This is paid from your account balance when you approve the transaction.
Can I undo this?
Once confirmed, it cannot be undone. However, you can change the project wallet later by calling initialize_wallets again with a different address.
What if the transaction fails?
If it fails, you can try again. The fee will be returned (no charge for failed transactions). Check the error message on KleverScan to understand what went wrong.
